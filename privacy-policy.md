# Privacy Policy — House Cooks

Last updated: 2026-05-03

## Who we are
House Cooks is an Afro house music brand operated by Kelvin Martinez. Contact: kelvmartin143@gmail.com.

## What this app does
The House Cooks app uses the Instagram Graph API to publish music videos (Reels and feed posts) to the official @housecooks Instagram account, and to read engagement (comments, likes) on those posts.

## What data we collect
- Instagram account ID and username for the @housecooks account only.
- Public engagement metrics on posts the app publishes (comment counts, like counts, comment text).
- Access tokens issued by Meta to authorize the app.

We do NOT collect data about Instagram users who view, like, or comment on House Cooks content beyond what Meta returns in the standard API response.

## How we use the data
- To publish music videos to the @housecooks Instagram account on a posting schedule.
- To read incoming comments so we can reply to fans.
- To track which posts performed well so we can post more of what fans like.

## How we store the data
- Access tokens are stored encrypted on a private server controlled by Kelvin Martinez.
- Engagement metrics are stored in a private database on the same server.
- Tokens and data are never shared with third parties.

## How long we keep the data
- Access tokens: until they are refreshed or the app is removed from the Instagram account.
- Engagement metrics: indefinitely for analytics. You can request deletion at any time.

## Your rights
You can request that we delete any data we hold about your account by emailing kelvmartin143@gmail.com. We will delete it within 30 days.

## Changes
We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will change.
